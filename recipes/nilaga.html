Beef Nilaga

Description

This is a recipe of Beef Nilaga

Ingredients

  • 1kg Pork Ribs
  • 8 cips water
  • 1 cube beef broth
  • 2 tbsp fish sauce
  • 1 tsp peppercorn
  • potato
  • cabbage
  • corn

Steps

  1. Prepare pork ribs by slicing them into individual rib
  2. On a large pot, put pork ribs, peppercorn, and water and boil for 1.5-2 hours
  3. Once the pork is cooked and soft, add the beef cube and fish sauce
  4. Add veggies and cook for 10-15 mins more
