Spaghetti

Description

This is a recipe of the classic Filo Spaghetti

Ingredients

  • 2 Cups DelMonte Tomato Sauce
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Minced Beef
  • Hotdog
  • 250g Pasta
  • 2 Tsp Sugar
  • Cheddar Cheese

Steps

  1. On a pan, cook sliced hotdogs. Set aside when cooked
  2. On the same pan, saute garlic and onion
  3. Add minced beef and cook until brown
  4. Add cooked hotdog
  5. Add tomato sauce. Cook for 10-15 mins
  6. Add grated cheese and sugar. Continue stirring
  7. Add cooked pasta, mix well and serve
