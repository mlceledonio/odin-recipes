Beef Pepper Rice

Description

Classic Beef Pepper Rice by Dawn

  • 2 tbs oyster sauce
  • 1 tbs soy sauce
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1 tbs mirin
  • Unli pepper
  • Sliced beef
  • 1/2 Onion
  • 2 cups rice
  • Corn (optional)
  1. On a bowl, combine oyster sauce, soy sauce, sugar, and mirin. Set aside
  2. Cook the onion. Once transluscent, add beef
  3. Cook for 3-5 mins then add the sauce
  4. Stir and make sure the beef is covered with sauce. Add rice and cook for 10 mins
  5. Add corn if available. Cook for 10 mins
  6. Season to taste
