Beef Pepper Rice

Description

Classic Beef Pepper Rice by Dawn

  • 2 tbs oyster sauce
  • 1 tbs soy sauce
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1 tbs mirin
  • Unli pepper
  • Sliced beef
  • 1/2 Onion
  • 2 cups rice
  • Corn (optional)
  1. On a bowl, combine oyster sauce, soy sauce, sugar, and mirin. Set aside
  2. Stir fry sliced beef. Set aside
  3. On the same pan, stir fry the rice
  4. Add cooked beef and pour the sauce
  5. Add corn if available. Cook for 10 mins
  6. Season to taste
